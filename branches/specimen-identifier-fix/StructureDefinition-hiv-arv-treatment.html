Resource Profile: ARV Treatment CarePlan

Official URL: http://openhie.org/fhir/rwanda-hiv/StructureDefinition/hiv-arv-treatment Version: 0.1.0
Active as of 2023-09-14 Computable Name: ARVTreatment

Represents a patient’s Antiretroviral Therapy (ART) treatment record, starting from ART initiation.

Usage:

  • Examples for this Resource Profile: CarePlan/ARVTreatmentInitiatedExample and CarePlan/ARVTreatmentRegimenChangedExample
